# Design System Documentation

This document outlines the core visual and interactive principles of our design system.

## Theme Overview

Our design system utilizes a **Dark Mode** for its interface, prioritizing visual comfort in low-light environments.

## Color Palette

The color palette is built around a distinct set of hues to create a cohesive and recognizable brand identity.

- **Primary Color:** `#00e00d` - This vibrant green is the cornerstone of our brand, used for primary calls to action, interactive elements, and key branding.
- **Secondary Color:** `#23891b` - A deeper, complementary green that supports the primary color, suitable for less prominent UI elements or secondary actions.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#ffb2bb` - A soft pink accent color, providing contrast and used for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
- **Neutral Color:** `#6f7a69` - A muted, earthy tone used for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, providing a calm and stable base.

## Typography

Our typographic system is designed for readability and visual hierarchy, using a consistent font family across various text roles.

- **Headline Font:** `Manrope` - Used for all major headings and titles, ensuring strong visual impact and readability.
- **Body Font:** `Manrope` - Applied to all paragraph text and longer content blocks, chosen for its clarity and legibility at various sizes.
- **Label Font:** `Manrope` - Employed for labels, captions, and smaller UI text, maintaining consistency with the overall typographic scheme.

## Shape and Form

The aesthetic of our UI elements is defined by a consistent approach to corner roundedness.

- **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate) - UI elements feature a moderate level of roundedness, providing a friendly yet professional feel without being overly soft or sharp.

## Spacing

The arrangement of elements and whitespace contributes significantly to the user experience, influencing readability and visual comfort.

- **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - Our system uses a normal level of spacing, offering a balanced density that ensures elements have adequate breathing room without feeling sparse or cramped.
